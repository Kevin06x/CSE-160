browser that supports "canvas"

Key Binds

W A S D - Move
Q E - Pan Left & Right
F - Add Block
R - Remove Block
Space - Move Up
Ctrl - Move Down
Click - Lock Mouse
Esc - Unlock Mouse

There seems to be a Koala nearby... walk up to it and see what's wrong.

Note: I capped the FPS to 60 as it was much more stable and the overall performance seems pretty good.
If you click within the canvas, you are able to lock your mouse and have the mouse rotation. Press [Esc] to reverse it.
I also added extra features like [Space] for elevation and [Ctrl] for lowering.
The story of this game is to get to the lost Koala and then bring him to the exit.
